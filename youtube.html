About YouTube

YouTube is a video-sharing platform created in 2005, which allows users to upload, share, and view videos on various topics.

It has become a significant source of entertainment, education, and information for users worldwide.

YouTube features a wide range of content, including music videos, vlogs, tutorials, and much more.

Key Features

  • Video Uploads: Users can upload their own videos to the platform.
  • Subscriptions: Allows users to subscribe to channels for updates.
  • Search and Recommendations: Helps users discover new content based on their interests.
  • Comments and Interactions: Enables community engagement through comments and likes.
  • Monetization: Allows content creators to earn from their videos through ads and other methods.
