About Instagram

Instagram is a social networking service owned by Meta Platforms, introduced in 2010.

It primarily focuses on photo and video sharing, enabling users to post and interact through visual content.

Instagram features Stories, IGTV, Reels, and more, catering to different forms of visual communication and engagement.

Key Features

  • Feed: User's main timeline displaying posts from followed accounts.
  • Stories: Temporary visual content that lasts for 24 hours.
  • IGTV: Long-form, vertical video platform.
  • Reels: Short, entertaining videos.
  • Explore: Discover new content based on interests and connections.
