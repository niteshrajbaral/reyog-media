About TikTok

TikTok is a social media platform owned by the Chinese company ByteDance, launched in 2016 internationally.

It allows users to create and share short videos, often featuring music, filters, and various effects.

TikTok has gained immense popularity, especially among younger demographics, and has transformed how users engage with short-form video content.

Key Features

  • Short-Form Videos: Users can create videos up to 60 seconds.
  • Music and Effects: Access to a vast library of soundtracks and effects.
  • For You Page: Personalized content discovery based on user preferences.
  • Duets and Reactions: Collaborative and response features for videos.
  • TikTok Challenges: Trending activities for user participation.
