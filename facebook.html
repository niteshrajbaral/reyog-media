About Facebook

Facebook is a social networking service launched in February 2004, founded by Mark Zuckerberg with his college roommates and fellow students.

It allows users to create a personal profile, add other users as friends, exchange messages, post status updates, share photos, videos, and more.

Facebook has evolved over the years, introducing features like Marketplace, Events, Groups, and Pages for businesses and organizations.

Key Features

  • Profile: Users can create profiles with personal information and photos.
  • News Feed: The central feature, showing updates from friends and pages followed.
  • Groups: Communities for shared interests.
  • Pages: Created by businesses, organizations, and public figures.
  • Messaging: Private communication between users.
